FAQ

1 - This application warns me when I am in a danger zone?

Yes, this is one of the main functions of this application. To be notified you should have GPS activated on your device and an internet connection available.

2 - How do I know what is the nearest police station?

You should press "Closest Police Station".

3 - How can I call to the emergency number?

At the bottom of the application screen is always available an SOS button with this function. After pressing the button you only have to confirm the call.

4 - I lost my passport ... and now?

In this case, you should press, "I had a problem" in the initial menu and then should, in this case, choose "I lost my passport."

5 - What kind of care should I have when I park my car?

For this type of information, you should press "Safety Advice" in the initial menu and then "In the car," in this case.

6 - I need the closest Hospital, what to do?

In the Home menu, you must click "Useful contacts" then you must select the filter to show only hospitals (icon at top right) and automatically appear hospitals in ascending order of distance from your current location.

7 - How do you change the application language?

In applying the lower bar has a designated settings (sprocket) button, pressing will be taken to a new screen. Then you have a box to be selected for you to choose a new language.

8 - Can I disable the notifications set by the application?

Yes, through the application settings (bottom bar of the application).

9 - I was robbed, what should I do?

In this case you should press "I was crime victim" and then "I was robbed." There you will have at your disposal all the advice to proceed.

10 - This application saves my personal data (location ...)?

No, this application protects all  users and will save only user’s country and language for future application improvements.
